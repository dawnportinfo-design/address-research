# Comparative Administration, Geopolitics, and Logistics

Address research needs comparative methods because address systems are shaped by law, administration, roads, geography, postal institutions, language, and conflict.

## Research Tasks

1. Compare administrative hierarchy across countries.
2. Separate technical addressing from political sovereignty claims.
3. Model postal-code absence, weakness, and maturity.
4. Connect geography, logistics, and data governance.

## Safety Boundary

Use neutral technical scopes and clearly mark unresolved or disputed cases.
